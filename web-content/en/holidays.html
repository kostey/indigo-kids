Holidays – summer camps

Come and join us on the way around the world with Mr Willy Fog.

Each week we will explore different countries and learn about their traditions. We might even taste some of the local cuisine. Every week, there will be one full day trip and swimming in our swimming pool. Once a week we`ll visit local swimming pool Podoli. We will go out and explore the culture life and sometimes small theatre groups will visit us.We will talk and play mainly in English to be able to communicate everywhere as proper travellers.

Sight-seeing tour is prepared for all two to nine year old children who like having fun. Mr Fog managed his trip around the world in 80 days. We will travel the whole summer holiday except for the last August week. You can join us for the whole ride or just for a week.

1.-5.7. Our journey will start in Egypt. We will visit pyramids and camels, cook local bread and learn to carry water jars on our heads. We will also learn what it is like to live in sarcophagi. Then we will visit South Africa. We will learn traditional African dances and there will be a great drums workshop.

8.-12.7. The following week, we will get to India and Tibet, we will learn local dances and special drawing techniques. We will create a huge mandala. We will be visited by native Tibetians who will theach us to wear sari. Later, we will all make a sari for ourselves.

15.-19.7. The third week, we will travel to China, Vietnam and Indonesia, we will create our own chopsticks and we will learn to eat with them. We will be visited by a theatre group that will play an Indonesian shadow puppet show and Vietnamese water puppet show for us. We will also create our own water puppets and then we will play with them in Polodli swimming pool.

22.-26.7. The next week, we will admire Japanese landscapes and sea creatures. We will cook local noodles and we will try to cook sushi.

29.7.- 2.8. In August we will arrive to the United States of America and meet Indians and cowboys. We will make a totem pole and Indian headbands. We will learn to use lasso and ride a horse. We will learn how to survive in the wild nature and everything that every Indian and cowboy should know.

5.-9.8. We will visit a continent called Australia. We will meet kangaroos and a Tasmanian worm and we will enjoy the ocean.

12.-16.8. The last week, we will travel back to Europe. We will go to the Great Britain – Willy Fog`s home. We will visit the British Royal Family. We will also visit other European countries.

You will be regularly informed about our adventures through our websites. We will take photos and draw pictures and put those in our traveller`s diary. The price list is here.
