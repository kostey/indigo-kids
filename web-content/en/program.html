8:00–9:00 arrival, free time
Our playroom is created to encourage children to play and learn. All our toys, kits and didactic gadgets are easilly accessible for children with a permanent place for them. It makes it easier for children to put the toys back after they played with them.

9:00–9:20 snack time (BIO quality)

9:20h–9:40h circle time
Children learn the program of the day, talk about their prevous day, learn a poem, song or a game. They also revise what they already know. Children learn to communicate with each other. They learn to listen to others as well as to be heard. They broaden their vocabulary range and they improve their pronunciation.

9:40–12:00 organised time based on the day`s theme
Art, pre-school education, sports, music, activities with a stress on learning and making their own decisions, outside time, wakls, trips.

12:00–12:30 lunch (BIO quality)

​12:30–13:00 takling about theday
